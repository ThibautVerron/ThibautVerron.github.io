About me

I am currently a post-doc in computer science in the APO team at the IRIT (ENSEEIHT, Toulouse, France).

I did my PhD thesis in the PolSys team in the PolSys team, in the LIP6 (UPMC, Paris, France), under supervision by Jean-Charles Faugère and Mohab Safey El Din. My thesis was about the algorithmic resolution of polynomial systems, and more precisely, the regularity of the computations of Gröbner bases for structured systems. I have been working on Gröbner basis algorithms for weighted homogeneous systems, and on a real roots classification problem for determinantal systems.

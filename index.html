About me

I am a PhD student in computer science at the laboratoire d'informatique de Paris 6 (LIP6), supervised by Jean-Charles Faugère and Mohab Safey El Din.

My thesis is about algorithmic resolution of polynomial systems, and more precisely, the computation of Gröbner bases for structured systems. I have been working on Gröbner basis algorithms for weighted homogeneous systems, and on a real roots classification problem for determinantal systems.
